Mahendra Singh Dhoni is an Indian former international cricketer who was captain of the Indian national cricket team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2017 and in Test cricket from 2008 to 2014. He led India to three ICC trophies, the most for any Indian captain. He is a right handed wicket-keeper batsman; as a batsman, he was most successful in limited overs cricket, scoring over 10,000 runs in One Day Internationals, with a reputation as one of the best finishers in the game.
